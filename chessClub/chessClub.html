Our Timeline

From a group of nerds to this thriving community

  • 2009-2011

    Our Humble Beginnings

    In 2009, the idea of establishing a chess club began to take shape amongst enthusiasts.

    By 2010, regular gatherings were organized, attracting a small but dedicated group of members.

  • March 2011

    An Agency is Born

    Recognizing the need for a more formal organization, the chess club officially registered as an agency in March 2011. This marked a significant milestone as it provided the club with legal recognition and allowed for more structured operations

  • December 2015

    Transition to Full Service

    By 2015, the chess club had become a prominent fixture in the local community, known for its inclusive atmosphere and quality events. To meet the growing demand for chess-related activities, the club transitioned to offering a full range of services.

  • July 2020

    Phase Two Expansion

    As the club continued to flourish, plans were made for a significant expansion in July 2020, marking the beginning of Phase Two.
