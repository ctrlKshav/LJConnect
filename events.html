Lumina 2024

Lumina 2024 was a spectacular cultural extravaganza that took place on LJ Campus. It was an evening filled with mesmerizing performances, delicious cuisine, and a celebration of diversity and unity within our LJ Connect community. From captivating dance routines to soul-stirring musical performances, Lumina 2024 showcased the incredible talent and cultural richness of our community. Thank you to everyone who attended and made this event a resounding success!

LJ Innovation Vllage

LJ Innovation Village, our premier technical event, was held on LJ Campus. It brought together innovators, tech enthusiasts, and aspiring entrepreneurs for a day of exploration, learning, and collaboration. Attendees had the opportunity to engage in hands-on workshops, participate in hackathons, and hear from industry experts about the latest trends and innovations. LJ Innovation Village sparked creativity, inspired innovation, and paved the way for future breakthroughs. Thank you to all the participants and partners who made this event a huge success!

Carpe-Diem

Carpe-Diem, LJ Connect's exhilarating sports event, took place on LJ Campus. It was a day filled with intense competition, thrilling matches, and unforgettable moments on the field. Participants showcased their skills and sportsmanship in a variety of sports, including basketball, soccer, and volleyball. Congratulations to all the participants for their dedication and passion, and thank you to everyone who supported and cheered on the teams!
